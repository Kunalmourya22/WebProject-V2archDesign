Project Overview

This landscape and garden design project by v2archdesign transforms an outdoor space into a structured, beautiful, and functional environment. Our approach integrates site analysis, planting design, hardscape elements, and water features to create a landscape that complements the architecture and enhances the quality of life for its users.

The project reflects our belief that great architecture extends beyond the building walls — the spaces between buildings matter just as much as the buildings themselves.

Design Elements

  • Masterplan and site layout developed from detailed topographic survey
  • Planting design with native and low-maintenance species
  • Pedestrian pathways, seating areas, and open lawns
  • Rainwater harvesting and irrigation planning
  • Lighting design for evening ambience and security
  • Material palette coordinated with the main building
