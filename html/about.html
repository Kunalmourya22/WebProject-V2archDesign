Director & Principal Architect, v2archdesign

Over 14+ years of experience in the field of architecture — encompassing design, team management, and project execution across multiple organisations. Throughout this period, an uncompromising emphasis on quality and client satisfaction has defined every engagement.

A highly competent team of professionals in Structural Engineering and Architecture collaborate through frequent brainstorming sessions at every stage of project development. Together, we stride confidently towards excellence in architecture, planning, and project management.

Architect Abhay Rajan holds a Bachelor of Architecture degree from the Malviya National Institute of Technology, Jaipur (Batch of 2008).
